Bulldog Taxi

Never wait for the bus again...

If you have taken CT Limo, you know how frustrating getting to Bradley can be. With Bulldog Taxi, you can reserve a ride to or from the airport in advance. Ride in comfort, and do not waste time waiting for a crowded bus. If you ride with at least two other people, you will even save money. It is that simple.
